*Last updated: 18 June 2026*

This policy explains how the **Cuesheet** app handles your data. Cuesheet is
published by **Shelterwood AI Inc.** ("Shelterwood", "we", "us"). For the
website, see our [company privacy policy](/privacy/).

Cuesheet is built to work on your device. The short version: the classes you
build and the work you do in Cuesheet stay on your device. We do not run our
own servers that collect your class data, and Cuesheet contains no third-party
advertising or analytics SDKs.

## What stays on your device

The classes you build in Cuesheet — your segments, effort and cadence settings,
durations, and the playlists you line up — are stored on your device.

When Cuesheet helps you build a class, that help runs on your device using
Apple's on-device Foundation Models. The content of your classes is processed
on the device and is not sent to Shelterwood to do this.

**Cuesheet does not transmit your class data to Shelterwood's servers.**

## Music and Apple MusicKit

Cuesheet uses **Apple MusicKit** to let you work with your own music library
and playlists. Your use of Apple Music and your music library is governed by
Apple's own terms and privacy policy, and access happens through Apple's
frameworks on your device. Cuesheet asks for permission to access your music;
you can grant or refuse this in iOS settings. We do not receive a copy of your
music library.

## What we do not collect

- We do not run third-party advertising SDKs in Cuesheet.
- We do not run third-party analytics or tracking SDKs in Cuesheet.
- We do not sell your data. We have nothing of yours to sell.
- We do not require you to create a Shelterwood account to use the app.

If this changes in a future version — for example, if we add an optional
feature that needs to send data off the device — we will update this policy and
make the change clear in the app before it takes effect.

## Apple's standard services

Cuesheet runs on Apple's platforms and uses standard Apple services. If you
have iCloud backup enabled, your device backup is handled by Apple under
Apple's terms. App downloads, purchases, and crash or diagnostic reports you
choose to share with Apple are handled by Apple, not by us.

## Deleting your data

Because your Cuesheet data lives on your device, you control it. You can delete
individual classes in the app, and removing the app from your device removes
the data Cuesheet stored on it. As we do not hold your class data on our own
servers, there is nothing further for us to delete on your behalf.

## Age

Cuesheet is intended for users aged 13 and over. It is not directed at children
under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from them.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will change the
"last updated" date above.
