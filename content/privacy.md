*Last updated: 18 June 2026*

This policy explains how **Shelterwood AI Inc.** ("Shelterwood", "we", "us")
handles personal data in connection with this website, **shelterwood.ai**. Our
products have their own privacy policies; for Cuesheet, see the
[Cuesheet privacy policy](/portfolio/cuesheet/privacy/).

## What we collect on this website

This is a simple, static website. We do not ask you to create an account, and
we do not run advertising or third-party analytics or tracking SDKs on it.

Like almost any website, the service that hosts shelterwood.ai records
standard technical information when a page is served — for example your IP
address, the page requested, the date and time, and your browser type. This is
ordinary server log data, used to operate and secure the site. We do not use
it to build a profile of you, and we do not sell it.

If you choose to email us, we will hold your message and contact details for as
long as we need them to reply and keep a reasonable record of the exchange.

## Cookies

We do not set tracking or advertising cookies. Any cookies present are limited
to those needed for the site to function (for example, remembering your light
or dark theme preference). They are stored only in your browser.

## How we use information

We use the limited information above to operate, secure, and improve the
website, and to respond to enquiries you send us. We do not sell personal data.

## Sharing

We do not sell or rent personal data. We share information only with the
service providers that host and deliver this website, and only as needed to run
it, or where we are required to by law.

## Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete
the personal data we hold about you, or to ask how we use it. To make a
request, email us at the address below and we will respond within a reasonable
time.

## Children

This website is intended for a general, adult audience. It is not directed at
children, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children through
it.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will change the
"last updated" date above.
