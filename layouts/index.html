What we do

We design, build, and operate our own software products. Each one is built AI-native from the ground up: the intelligence is part of the foundation, not a feature bolted on at the end. The result is software that does more of the tedious work for you and stays out of the way of the rest.

Our approach is deliberate. We favour a small number of carefully made products over a long list of half-finished ones, and we measure success by whether the people who use our software get their work done faster and with less friction.

Our flagship: Cuesheet

Cuesheet is an app for indoor-cycling and spin instructors. Plan a class on paper or in your head today; Cuesheet turns it into a structured plan you can run hands-free from the bike. Build the ride, set the intervals to the music, and let the app cue each block so you can teach instead of watch the clock.

It is the first product to come out of Shelterwood, and the model for how we build: a real tool for real people, made well.

Why "Shelterwood"?

In forestry, a shelterwood is a stand of mature trees left in place to protect the next generation of growth until the seedlings are strong enough to stand on their own. The name reflects how we work: stewardship over speed, patience over hype, and craft that compounds over time.
